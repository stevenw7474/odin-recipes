Lets make some toast!

Ingredients

  • Toast
  • Spread of your choice (butter, cream cheese, peanut butter, etc.)
  • Competent Toaster (individual making the toast)
  • Toaster Machine

Steps

  1. Open package of bread and pull out a piece of bread. If bread is not sliced, slice to your liking
  2. Insert bread into toaster and set temperature setting to manufacturer's recommended level
  3. Once toast is heated to your liking, remove from toaster and apply spread to your liking
